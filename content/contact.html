Contribute

Here is some way to contribute to the project.

  • Edit the bibliography resource;
  • Review the Workflows available and suggest comments or improvement (or do similar workflows in python);
  • Add data sets;
  • Suggest ideas to organize a data challenge;
  • Improve the FAQ with more questions;
  • Improve this website.

Contact

If you have comments, suggestions or questions about this platform or if you wish to submit some of your own material on missing values (references, tutorials, data sets, etc.) please feel free to either submit an issue on GitHub, or to contact us by using the form below (which will send us an e-mail at: r-miss-tastic [at] clementine [dot] wf).
